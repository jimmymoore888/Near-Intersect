## 1. Abstract

Near Intersect Volcano is a development-stage rules-based protocol design intended for NEAR Protocol.

The system is built around stablecoin pressure accounting, deterministic treasury routing, milestone-based eruption logic, long-term lock behavior, and purchasing-power-aware doctrine through the OutpaceInflation Module, also called OIM.

Near Intersect Volcano is not live as a full public on-chain financial system until the matching Rust contract, WASM artifact, contract account, source hash, explorer link, audit status, and legal review are publicly verified.

This whitepaper describes the intended system architecture.

It does not claim that all features are currently live.

It does not guarantee profit, returns, treasury growth, token value, purchasing-power protection, or the occurrence of any future eruption milestone.
