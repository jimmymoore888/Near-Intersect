Distribution Cycle Rule

A milestone is a pressure threshold, not a guaranteed payout. The final deployed contract must define exactly what portion routes to treasury, growth, reserve, operations, volcano pressure, and any future eruption pool. The public rule is simple: pressure builds, threshold is reached, eruption cycle becomes eligible, then the next milestone rises.
